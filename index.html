About RideLanka

RideLanka is a smart and reliable transport solution designed to make commuting safer, more efficient, and stress-free for students and office staff. Whether you need to find the nearest school or staff transport, book a ride effortlessly, or view available seats in real-time, RideLanka ensures a seamless travel experience. With AI-powered price prediction, drivers can list their services at competitive rates, while parents receive instant notifications when their child is dropped off safely. Our automated attendance marking guarantees accountability, and the AI chatbot provides 24/7 assistance for any queries. RideLanka is more than just a transport service—it’s your trusted companion for a smooth and secure journey!
