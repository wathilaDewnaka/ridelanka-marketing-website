About RideLanka

RideLanka is a smart and reliable transport solution designed to make commuting safer, more efficient, and stress-free for students and office staff.

  • Find the nearest school or staff transport
  • Book a ride effortlessly
  • View real-time seat availability
  • Get instant notifications when your child is dropped off safely
  • AI-powered price prediction for competitive rates
  • 24/7 AI chatbot support for instant assistance
